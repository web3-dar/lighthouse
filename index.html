Our Projects. Support Us

At the Light House Giving Foundation, we are committed to making a tangible difference in communities affected by disaster and hardship. Our projects focus on providing emergency relief, rebuilding lives, and offering sustainable solutions for those in need.

California Wildfires Recovery

After devastating wildfires ravaged California, we’ve been working tirelessly to offer relief in the form of emergency shelters, rebuilding homes, and providing psychological support to those who lost everything.

Florida Flood Recovery

In response to the catastrophic flooding in Florida, we’ve focused on providing clean water, rebuilding infrastructure, and offering financial support to displaced families.

Shelter Giving

Every contribution, no matter how small, helps give someone a safe place to live and a chance for a better tomorrow. Help us provide shelter, restore dignity, and bring hope to those who need it most.

Hurricane Katrina Rebuilding

Over a decade after the storm, we continue to support ongoing rebuilding efforts in New Orleans, ensuring that affected communities have access to permanent housing, education, and resources for long-term recovery.

Tornado Recovery in Oklahoma

Working with local communities in Oklahoma to rebuild homes and provide critical resources to families affected by destructive tornadoes.

Medical Mission in Southern Kenya

Sep. 15th 21

In Southern Kenya, the Lighthouse Giving Foundation has been working with local healthcare providers to deliver critical medical supplies and health services to remote areas. The mission aims to provide free medical care to those who have limited access to hospitals and clinics. During the trip, over 1,500 people received consultations, vaccinations, and treatments, making a huge impact on the local community.

Florida Flood Recovery Efforts

Oct. 10th 14

After the devastating floods in Florida, our team worked around the clock to provide aid to affected families. We focused on distributing food, clean water, and emergency supplies, while also helping to rebuild homes and restore lost livelihoods. Our collaboration with local organizations allowed us to provide immediate relief and long-term support for those impacted by the disaster.

Hurricane Relief Efforts in New Orleans

Nov. 2nd 9

In the wake of the recent hurricane season, the Lighthouse Giving Foundation has been committed to providing much-needed relief to the communities of New Orleans. From food distribution to offering temporary shelter, we have been on the ground working with local leaders to ensure families have the resources they need to recover. The rebuilding efforts are ongoing, and we remain dedicated to helping the city rebuild stronger and more resilient than ever before.
